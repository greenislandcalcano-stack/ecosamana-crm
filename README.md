# EcoSamana CRM

> Professional Booking & Operations Management System for EcoSamana Adventures

---

## Overview

EcoSamana CRM is a custom-built reservation and operations platform developed specifically for EcoSamana Adventures.

Unlike generic CRM software, this system is designed around the daily workflow of a tour operator in Samaná, Dominican Republic.

Its purpose is to manage every aspect of the business from one centralized application.

---

## Main Modules

- 📅 Reservations
- 👥 Customers
- 💳 Payments
- 🚐 Daily Tours
- 👨‍✈️ Guides
- 🚗 Drivers
- ⭐ Reviews
- 📊 Reports
- ⚙️ Settings
